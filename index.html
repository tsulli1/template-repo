Designed for explorers of all ages

Celestial Explorer combines approachable storytelling with accurate planetary data. Whether you are guiding a classroom or learning solo, you are just a click away from orbit.

Guided discovery

Click any planet to uncover quick facts and vivid descriptions. Each world has a dedicated info panel with hand-picked highlights.

AI-powered tales

Use the built-in Gemini prompts to spin short fiction or reveal hidden trivia about your favourite planet. Perfect for sparking imagination.

What makes the experience special?

Smooth 3D controls

Pan, zoom, and orbit effortlessly with mouse or touch controls powered by Three.js.

Immersive ambience

Dynamic lighting, shimmering stars, and ambient colours create a cosmic backdrop for your journey.

Accessible insights

Clear typography and responsive layout ensure the solar system feels great on desktops and tablets alike.
